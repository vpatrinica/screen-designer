Fan Visualization with RPM Gauges

Instructions:

Click on the fan power buttons to toggle them on/off. When a fan is on:

  • The power symbol turns green
  • The background glows with a pulsing light blue effect
  • The fan blades rotate continuously
  • The RPM gauge shows 3600 RPM

When a fan is off:

  • The power symbol turns gray
  • The background is dark
  • The fan blades stop rotating
  • The RPM gauge shows 0 RPM
